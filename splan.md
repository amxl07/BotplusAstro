# BotPlus SEO, ICP, GTM, And Website Strategy Plan

Last updated: 2026-07-06

## Active Safety Override

This document supersedes older BotPlus page-list-first, avatar-first, call-center-first, and broad enterprise-platform positioning.

BotPlus public copy must not use unqualified claims such as:

- AI that is indistinguishable from humans.
- Human replacement.
- Call center replacement.
- 60% or 80% guaranteed cost savings.
- Perfect consistency.
- Emotion reading as a primary claim.
- Hidden AI or "customers will not know it is AI."
- Fully autonomous service resolution.
- Guaranteed no queues, no staff load, no implementation effort, or no workflow change.
- Security, compliance, or certification claims unless verified.

Use scoped, operational language instead:

- approved content
- guided service journeys
- kiosk and self-service workflows
- multilingual support
- staff handoff
- consent and data boundaries
- workflow-specific pilots
- existing-system integration where approved and technically available

## 1. Strategic Decision

### Final Direction

BotPlus should be repositioned as a product, not the master enterprise platform.

Recommended product role:

```text
BotPlus is the governed AI kiosk and interactive service experience product for high-touch physical and digital service locations.
```

Operational category:

```text
AI-powered interactive kiosk and self-service experience platform
```

Plain-language category for sales:

```text
AI kiosk and digital service guide for service centers, venues, hospitality, retail showrooms, and events.
```

### Why This Direction

DentDesk is working because it is narrow, operational, and buyer-specific. It does not sell "AI automation" first. It sells a specific paid-pilot wedge with safe boundaries.

BotPlus should copy that discipline:

- specific buyer
- specific physical/digital service environment
- specific pilot
- specific outcome
- specific governance story

BotPlus should not become the global enterprise platform because Entellex owns that lane with stronger positioning:

```text
Entellex = the control platform / controlled execution layer for enterprise AI.
```

BotPlus should not become contact-center or support automation because ResolveDesk owns that lane:

```text
ResolveDesk = AI workflow-resolution layer for contact centers and support operations.
```

BotPlus should not become a dental product because DentDesk owns that lane:

```text
DentDesk = dental front-desk overflow, after-hours, and missed-call coverage.
```

### Final Brand Architecture

| Brand | Role | Owns | Avoids |
|---|---|---|---|
| Entellex | Master enterprise platform | controlled execution, governance, cross-system AI operations | narrow kiosk SEO, contact-center keyword capture |
| ResolveDesk | AI frontline for support/contact centers | AI call center, contact centre, support automation, workflow resolution | kiosk/avatar/branch experience SEO |
| DentDesk | Dental patient-access wedge | dental overflow calls, after-hours dental answering, missed-call capture | generic clinic chatbot or kiosk terms |
| BotPlus | AI kiosk and interactive service experience product | interactive kiosk, self-service kiosk, AI kiosk, avatar kiosk, visitor/queue/wayfinding/event/hospitality journeys | support queues, contact center automation, enterprise platform ownership |

### BotPlus Vs AvatarPlus

The Entellex docs currently use `AvatarPlus` for avatar/kiosk/branch/event interfaces. The old live site uses `BotPlus` as the brand.

Do not sell `BotPlus` and `AvatarPlus` as two separate products to the same buyer.

Recommended resolution:

- Use `BotPlus` as the public product/site brand for the existing domain and GCC traffic.
- Treat `AvatarPlus` as the Entellex interface capability or module inside BotPlus, unless the Entellex product library is deliberately changed.
- On Entellex, describe it as `BotPlus / AvatarPlus interfaces` during transition.
- On botplus.ae and botplus.tech, lead with `BotPlus`.

## 2. Product And Positioning

### What BotPlus Does

BotPlus helps organizations deploy AI-guided kiosk, avatar, web, WhatsApp, and service-desk journeys for front-of-house service environments.

Typical workflows:

- answer approved service questions
- guide users to the right counter, queue, desk, room, gate, store, booth, or staff member
- collect the minimum context needed for the next step
- register visitors or event attendees
- issue or route queue tickets
- explain documents, requirements, hours, directions, and next steps
- hand off to staff with context
- continue the journey through WhatsApp, web, email, or staff follow-up
- provide analytics on questions, drop-offs, handoffs, queue/service patterns, and usage

### What It Really Is

```text
A governed service interface for locations where visitors need fast guidance, routing, self-service, or staff handoff.
```

### What It Is Not

- Not an avatar generator.
- Not a video-avatar creation tool.
- Not a generic chatbot.
- Not a contact-center automation product.
- Not a CCaaS replacement.
- Not a full enterprise AI platform.
- Not a staff-replacement pitch.
- Not a kiosk hardware reseller only.
- Not digital signage only.

### Core Insight

```text
The value is not that an avatar looks human.
The value is that a visitor can complete a service journey faster, in the right language, through approved content, with staff handoff when needed.
```

### Main Positioning

```text
BotPlus turns kiosks, screens, service desks, and digital touchpoints into governed AI-guided service journeys.
```

Expanded:

```text
BotPlus helps high-touch service locations guide visitors through approved self-service, information, queue, check-in, wayfinding, and handoff workflows. It can run through touch-screen kiosks, avatar interfaces, web journeys, WhatsApp, and staff dashboards while keeping content, data capture, escalation, and analytics under control.
```

### What BotPlus Replaces Or Reduces

BotPlus does not replace the front desk or service staff.

It can reduce:

- static information kiosks that cannot answer follow-up questions
- repeated front-desk questions
- unstructured visitor routing
- manual visitor registration
- manual event lead capture
- staff time spent explaining the same service requirements
- missed context when a visitor is handed from kiosk to staff
- disconnected kiosk, web, WhatsApp, and staff interactions

Kiosk is the stronger SEO root.

Avatar is a differentiator.

## 16. Content And Copy Rules

### Rule 1: Kiosk First, Avatar Second

Weak:

```text
Photorealistic avatars that feel human.
```

Better:

```text
AI-guided kiosk journeys that answer approved questions, route visitors, and hand off to staff with context.
```

### Rule 2: Do Not Sell Staff Replacement

Weak:

```text
Say goodbye to expensive human agents.
```

Better:

```text
Give front-desk and service teams a controlled self-service layer for repetitive visitor questions and routing.
```

### Rule 3: Do Not Overclaim Emotion Recognition

Weak:

```text
Reads customer emotion in real time.
```

Better:

```text
Escalates unclear, sensitive, or frustrated interactions to staff with context.
```

### Rule 4: Make Governance Commercial

Governance is not a compliance footnote. It is how BotPlus becomes enterprise-grade.

Every major page should explain:

- approved content
- scoped data capture
- integration permissions
- human handoff
- audit/analytics
- fallback behavior

### Rule 5: Use Concierge Carefully

Use `concierge` in:

- hotel pages
- premium guest-experience copy
- venue/mall visitor experience when natural

Do not use it as:

- the root category
- service-center SEO root
- government page primary keyword unless buyer data proves it

### Rule 6: Every Page Needs A Buyer Job

If a page cannot answer:

```text
Who is searching this, what do they need to decide, and what action should they take?
```

Do not build it.
